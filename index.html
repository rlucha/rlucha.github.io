Their platform was built around Magento and my role was to design a responsive SASS framework with the main priority of being easy to maintain by non CSS experts and very modular to handle future scenarios.

The main milestones achieved were:

The tools I used in this projects were: axure, SASS, BEM, angularJs, inVision, grunt and assemble.io
